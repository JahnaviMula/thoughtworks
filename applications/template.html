Introduction

Technology has dramatically changed the way people work. With the rise of computers, automation, and digital tools, many traditional jobs have evolved or disappeared, while new career opportunities have emerged.

    Before Technology

  • Work done manually
  • Jobs were local
  • Repetitive tasks done by humans
  • Limited job opportunities

    After Technology

  • Work is automated
  • Jobs can be remote
  • Machines handle repetitive tasks
  • Expanded job opportunities
